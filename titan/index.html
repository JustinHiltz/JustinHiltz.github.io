New Home Construction

In most cases, new homes offer advantages that are simply not available in a resale property. With a new home from Titan Homes and Design:

  • You know what you want
  • We build what you want
  • You know what your costs will be

In addition, when you buy a new home or condominium in Ontario, it comes with a comprehensive statutory warranty. Tarion Warranty Corporation is the regulator of the new home building industry in Ontario. It licenses all new home and condominium builders in the province and ensures that all new homeowners receive the new home warranty coverage they are entitled to by law.

Our knowledge gained through hands-on experience can be put to work for you. The Titan New Home Team can be an invaluable asset in helping you with decisions that affect the designing of your new home, whether using one of our designs or working in conjunction with your architect.
